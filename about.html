At KKCulture (formerly known as KKCompany), we are building Asia’s most adventurous content innovation company. Based in Taipei, our portfolio includes KKFARM, KKLIVE, KKTV, and a diverse array of innovative ventures that are actively shaping the creator economy.

 

Founded by Chris Lin and Lambert Chien, we have a rich history dating back to 2004, when we launched KKBOX, the world’s first legal music streaming service. Over the past 18 years, we have evolved into one of the most dynamic media technology companies in the Asia-Pacific region, providing creators and storytellers with cutting-edge platforms that empower them to reach millions of new audiences.

 

As a company, we are dedicated to driving change in the industry, whether it’s through investing in content creation, powering live entertainment, using streaming technology to reimagine experiences, or making artificial intelligence more accessible to creators.

 

At KKCulture, we are committed to making technology and innovation easy and enjoyable for creators everywhere.
